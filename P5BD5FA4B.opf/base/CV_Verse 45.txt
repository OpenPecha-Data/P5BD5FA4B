atha tu pramāṇasiddhir bhavaty apekṣyaiva te prameyāṇi |vyatyaya evaṃ sati te dhruvaṃ pramāṇaprameyāṇāṃ
||45||

athāpi manyase ’pekṣyaiva prameyān arthān pramāṇāni bhavantīti mā bhūt pūrvokta doṣa iti kṛtvā,
evaṃ te sati vyatyayaḥ pramāṇaprameyāṇāṃ bhavati |pramāṇāni te prameyāṇi bhavanti prameyaiḥ
sādhitatvāt | prameyāṇi ca pramāṇāni bhavanti pramāṇānāṃ sādhakatvāt |
