kiṃ cānyat | yadi ca svabhāvataḥ syād grāhaḥ kastaṃ nivartayed grāhaṃ |śéṣeṣv apy eṣa vidhis
tasmād eṣo ’nupālambhaḥ ||67||

yadi ca mṛgatṛṣṇāyāṃ jalagrāhaḥ svabhāvataḥ syāt ka eva taṃ vinivartayet |na hi svabhāvaḥ śakyo
vinivartayituṃ yathāgner uṣṇatvam apāṃ dravatvam ākāśasya nirāvaraṇatvaṃ | dṛṣṭaṃ cāsya
vinivartanam |tasmāc chūnyasvabhāvo grāhaḥ | yathā caitad evaṃ śeṣeṣv api dharmeṣv eṣa kramaḥ
pratyavagantavyo grāhyaprabhṛtiṣu pañcasu |tatra yad bhavatoktaṃ ṣaṭkabhāvād aśūnyāḥ
sarvabhāvā iti tad na |yat punar bhavatoktaṃ hetoś ca te na siddhir naiḥ svābhāvyāt kuto
hi te hetuḥ | nirhetukasya siddhir na copapannāsya te ’rthasyeti || atra brūmaḥ |
