kiṃ cānyat |mā śabdavad ity etat syāt te buddhi na caitad upapannam |śabdena hy atra satā bhaviṣyato
vāraṇaṃ tasya ||3||

syāt te buddhiḥ, yathā nāma kaścid brūyān mā śabdaṃ kārṣīr iti svayam eva śabdaṃ kuryāt tena
ca śabdena tasya śabdasya vyāvartanaṃ kriyeta, evam eva śūnyāḥ sarvabhāvā iti śūnyena vacanena
sarvabhāvasvabhāvasya vyāvartanaṃ kriyata iti |atra vayaṃ brūmaḥ | etad apy anupapannam
| kiṃ kāraṇam | satā hy atra śabdena bhaviṣyataḥ śabdasya pratiṣedhaḥ kriyate |na punar
iha bhavataḥ satā vacanena sarvabhāvasvabhāvapratiṣedhaḥ kriyate | tava hi matena vacanam
apy asat, sarvabhāvasvabhāvo ’py asan | tasmād ayaṃ mā śabdavad iti viṣamopanyāsaḥ |
