atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyata itīdaṃ |āśaṅkitaṃ yad uktaṃ bhavaty anāśaṅkitaṃ
tac ca ||60||

na hi vayaṃ dharmāṇāṃ svabhāvaṃ pratiṣedhayāmo dharmavinirmuktasya vā kasyacid arthasya svabhāvam
abhyupagacchāmaḥ |nanv evaṃ sati ya upālambho bhavato yadi dharmā niḥsvabhāvāḥ kasya
khalv idānīm anyasyārthasya dharmavinirmuktasya svabhāvo bhavati sa yuktam upadeṣṭum iti
dūrāpakṛṣṭam evaitad bhavati, upālambho na bhavati |yat punar bhavatoktaṃ sata eva pratiṣedho
nāsti ghaṭo geha ity ayaṃ yasmāt | dṛṣtaḥ pratiṣedho ’yaṃ sataḥ svabhāvasya te tasmād
iti || atra brūmaḥ |
