sata eva pratiṣedho yadi śūnyatvaṃ nanu prasiddham idam |pratiṣedhayate hi bhavān bhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvam
||61||

yadi sata eva pratiṣedho bhavati nāsato bhavāṃś ca sarvabhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvaṃ pratiṣedhayati,
nanu pratisiddhaṃ sarvabhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvaṃ |tvadvacanena pratiṣedhasadbhāvān niḥsvabhāvatvasya
ca sarvabhāvānāṃ pratiṣiddhatvāt prasiddhā śūnyatā |
